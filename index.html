Our Story

THREE DECADES OF EXCELLENCE

Founded in 1992 and headquartered in Bhiwadi — one of India's fastest-growing corporate and industrial hubs — Praveen Traders has built an unmatched reputation for quality products, rapid delivery, and lifelong customer relationships.

Our Vision

To provide quality products and services for the industrial market at a PAN India level — making world-class industrial solutions accessible to every industry.

Our Mission

Customer satisfaction is our foremost priority. We understand your requirements and procure quality products from across the globe to serve the industrial market with Reliability, Authenticity, and Transparency.

Our Philosophy

We embrace "Kaizen" — continuous improvement. Every employee is encouraged to adopt new ideas and techniques. We work closely with customers to truly understand their needs.

Founded By

Late Mr. Laxmi Narayan Jhanwar

A visionary entrepreneur who built PTIS from the ground up with an unwavering belief in quality and customer care. His legacy lives on in every product we supply and every relationship we nurture.
